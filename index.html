Understanding Microplastic Pollution

Microplastics originate from larger plastic waste that degrades under sunlight and environmental stress.

📦

Sources

Plastic packaging, synthetic clothing fibers, tire wear particles, and industrial pellets.

🐠

Environmental Impact

Microplastics disrupt marine ecosystems, harm aquatic biodiversity, and enter food chains.

🫀

Human Health Risk

Microplastics enter the human body via food, water, and inhalation — with unknown long-term effects.

🏭

Industrial Contribution

Industrial discharge and single-use plastics are the primary contributors to microplastic pollution.
